Generative AI ft. Shrek memes

Welcome to our webpage! We are a group of students from Braunschweig University of Art, and we are excited to present to you our project on Generative AI.

You will ask us, what is special about our project? Well, we have combined the power of Generative AI with the magic of Shrek memes! During our work we cooperated with two independent AI to create the content, one that generates text and another that generates images, namely ChatGPT and AdobeFirefly.

Have you ever had this wild fever dreams during being sick? We used ChatGPT to generate plot of a dream like this and then we asked AdobeFirefly to visualize it. But this is not all! We used Shrek memes as a reference for the image generating AI. Which means that the generated images are inspired by Shrek memes. We hope you will enjoy the results as much as we do!We were feeding AdobeFirefly with dreams dividing them into parts to make it easier for the AI to catch all the important aspects of the dream. So we got a series of images that are forming the whole story. At the end we added a bit of human impact to create a collage of the generated images.

As we used Shrek memes as a reference, we decided that the final result should be also presented as a meme. We asked ChatGPT to help us with this. It created sayings we could use for our collage. We think it could be a great example of how person can interact with AI to create something new and unique!
